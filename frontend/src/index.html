How can I start?

  • 1

    There is a brand new challenge every day. Want to start from Day 1? No problem! Use the slide bar to find out previous challenges.

  • 2

    Find someone - a stranger, a co-worker, a neighbor, a friend or a loved one, and spread the love by completing the challenge in action.

  • 3

    Share it with the world. Post on Twitter and use #100DaysOfLoveChallenge.
